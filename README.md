## 🎯 The Problem

The NFT art market was built to serve whales, institutions, and speculative traders. The everyday digital artist has been priced out, pushed aside, and left behind.

| Pain Point | Reality |
|------------|---------|
| 💸 **High Fees** | Artists on Ethereum lose 75%+ of a $100 sale to platform fees + gas |
| 🚪 **Barriers to Entry** | 69% of illiquid NFT collections fall to a 0 ETH floor price |
| 🔒 **No Safety Net** | If art doesn't sell, artists absorb the total loss |
| 📈 **Speculation Over Art** | Platforms reward flipping and bot trading, not creativity |
| 😵 **Confusing UX** | Wallet setup, gas estimators, and Web3 jargon scare off new users |

**Bidtiful is the solution.**

## 🔑 Core Features

### 🎟️ Bid Credit System — *Our Core Innovation*

The single most important innovation in Bidtiful's architecture. No gas anxiety. No hidden fees. Just bidding.

| Step | How It Works |
|------|-------------|
| **Buy Credits** | Purchase bid credits with TON. Sliding scale pricing — the more you buy, the bigger your bonus (up to 100% extra). |
| **Place Bids** | Each bid costs exactly **1 credit**. No variable gas fees. One credit = one bid. Period. |
| **Lose an Auction** | **100% of credits returned.** Zero risk in bidding. |
| **Win an Auction** | Credits spent, NFT delivered to your wallet. Artist keeps **100%** of the sale price. |
| **Gas Fees** | **Covered by the platform.** You never see or pay gas fees. |

### ⚡ Sudden Death Mode

When the auction timer drops below **30 seconds**, each new bid resets the clock. No sniping — the last bidder standing wins. Pure auction drama.

### 🛡️ Quick Sell — *The Artist Safety Net*

**This feature exists nowhere else in the NFT ecosystem.**

If an artwork doesn't sell within its first 3 auction displays, the artist gets the option to sell directly to Bidtiful at floor price. It's completely optional — but it means **no artist ever leaves empty-handed.**

### 🎨 Curated Quality

Bidtiful is for serious artists only. No memes, no spam, no low-effort content.

- ✅ Original digital art — painting, illustration, 3D, generative, photography
