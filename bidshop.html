Common Questions
How does the bonus work? ▼
For every 1 TON you spend, you earn a 1% bonus on your total bids. Spend 10 TON and get 10% bonus (110 bids instead of 100). At 100 TON, you get 100% bonus — doubling your bids to 2,000!
Can I convert credits back to TON? ▼
No. Bid credits are non-refundable and cannot be converted back to cryptocurrency. However, if you lose an auction, all credits you spent on bids in that auction are returned to your balance.
What happens to my credits if I win? ▼
When you win an auction, the credits you spent on bids are consumed and you receive the artwork as an NFT in your wallet. The artwork is yours forever.
Do credits expire? ▼
No! Your bid credits never expire. Use them whenever you're ready — no rush.
Who pays the gas fees? ▼
Bidtiful covers all blockchain gas fees. Your credit purchase price is the only cost — no hidden fees, no surprises.
